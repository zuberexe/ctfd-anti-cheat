CTFd Anti-Cheat — Settings

All thresholds live in the cm_config table. Defaults are seeded on first plugin load and are documented inline in utils.py (search for DEFAULTS). Changes here are persisted immediately; you do not need to restart CTFd. To re-evaluate the corpus with new thresholds, hit "Run All Detectors" on the dashboard.

Forward-looking User-Agent capture

CTFd's Tracking table stores IPs but not User-Agents. Enable this to log (user, ip, ua) on every flag submission going forward. UA-based detectors (IP+UA overlap, session-swap) will upgrade from IP-only to IP+UA matching as soon as data accumulates. Historical UAs cannot be recovered.
